# Will Alston

I build AI systems that make sense of large, noisy, real-world data. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, data engineering, and applied analytics, where the problems are messy and the stakes are high.

Right now I'm focused on NLP and social intelligence at scale. I design pipelines that process hundreds of thousands of documents through transformer models, train custom classifiers to outperform commercial platforms, and turn the output into something a decision-maker can act on Monday morning.

## What I Build

**AI and NLP Systems**
Sentiment analysis, stance detection, emotion classification, toxicity scoring, named entity recognition, moral foundations analysis, sarcasm detection. I train custom distilBERT models and build multi-model ensembles that consistently beat vendor baselines. I care about accuracy, not just automation.

**Data Pipelines at Scale**
End-to-end systems that go from raw ingestion through model inference to executive-ready deliverables. I've built pipelines that process 780K+ documents, deduplicate intelligently, score across 10+ model layers, and output validated reports with consistent numbers throughout.

**Mobile and Web Products**
I ship real products, not just notebooks. I have apps published in both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including a gratitude journal with Apple Watch support, a wellness app built with Swift and SwiftUI, and a meditation app on Android. On the web side: a real-time collaborative writing tool with Firebase and live sync, a speech practice app that runs entirely on-device with zero API cost, a 3D portfolio with Three.js, a full Power BI mastery curriculum, a cost-of-living explorer, and an AI-powered gift finder. I build across the stack because ideas deserve to exist outside of a terminal.

**Analytics Platforms**
Interactive dashboards, automated reporting, and tools that let non-technical stakeholders explore data on their own terms. I've worked with Power BI, Streamlit, React, and custom desktop applications packaged for macOS with native Swift launchers.

**Full-Stack Data Infrastructure**
Cloud architecture on AWS, GCP, Snowflake, and BigQuery. ETL systems connecting Salesforce and enterprise platforms. I've led data governance projects cataloging thousands of critical data elements and built quality frameworks that hold up under audit.
